About me

Hi! My name is Paola and currently I'm working towards my Database Report Development and Database Analysis certificate at Bellevue College, WA. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration (Marketing and International Business, Universidad Autonoma de Occidente).

I love working with data because it allows for problem-solving, communication, insight, and the generation of business strategies. I find it intriguing and inspiring to work with data. I am excited to grow and continue learning on my journey as a data/business analyst. I value working with others, and, above all, I push myself to learn something new, whether about programming languages, data analysis tools, or random facts.

Before, I had worked for three years as a Project Executive in a market research company. I led several projects from inception to completion, including data analysis. This gives me the ability to translate complex questions into understandable insights.
